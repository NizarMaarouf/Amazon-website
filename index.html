4.0 out of 5 stars Unless you are an extremely thin woman, this will run quite small
Reviewed in the United States on December 19, 2018
Verified Purchase
Overall, this is a nice sweatshirt. There are some things to be aware of. This is not your typical unisex sweatshirt. It is short waisted which means that it will not cover your bum. It will sit pretty much at your waist or maybe even a little above if you are an above average height woman. Also, it is cut quite slim for a sweatshirt so it's more fitted and not your typical baggy sweatshirt. None of this is a bad thing but it is a more fashion forward style and not typical and should be made even more clear in the description. The material is heavy sweatshirt material but still soft which is nice and the burgundy color is lovely. I gave it 4 stars only because even given the fact that it's cut for women, it does seem to me to run fairly small. I asked my very slim 105 lb, 5' 6" daughter and she agreed. You will want to go up at least one size with this sweatshirt particularly if you don't have a super slim frame.
